DRAGONzine is doing well! DRAGONbooks is now set up... its a way to earn some more cash to help DRAGONzine get even better. Also, a new column has been started, DRAGONreviews for a monthly book review.

As for the stories of DRAGON's 6th issue... well, we've got some great ones. Dr. Sasha Cornelius in The Martian Virus is a unique story from me... Taking up three project credits at school, it is really cool! I wrote this for Biology to teach the six characteristics of living things.

Second, I have another, non-Azeroth story, Reality Bytes which is a humorous modern-day fantasy about Vampires. It also introduces an odd character of mine, Professor von Carpworth, the truly nutty Professor who was the first to get a Ph.D. in Psychoptrics, and has since pushed Chromatology, the study of Color, to new limits... Expect more from him, he is really funny!

We have two more stories from Ernest Stewart loaned to us... The God and The Trouble With Earthlings. A gentle warning, you have to read them all the way through to get the irony!

Last, but certainly not least, rounding off this collection of stories is Part 2 of 'The Sun Fox'.
