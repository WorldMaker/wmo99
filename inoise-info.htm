iNOISE is something that I am working on. Right now there are a bunch of formats and options to choose from. Normally, the author of the page chooses his or her favorite format and peice. I however beleive that the User decides to choose! When I noise is finished, it will offer lots of choices, it will also allow a worldwide network of collections so that if your favorite background music isn't in the current page's collection you can still hear it. Hopefully, iNOISE will even be smart enough to remember your choices from site to site.

Current way for placing background music:

.MOD: ModPlug with an EMBED tag
.MID: IE's BGSOUND or CakeWalk/Media Player
.WAV: IE's BGSOUND or Media Player
.MP3: Windows MediaPlayer

iNOISE will use ModPlug and Windows MediaPlayer and provide the the same thing, but it will DYNAMICALLY generate the EMBED tags per your choices, and through the iNOISE Browser allow you to play the music in a seperate window and have full control of it.
